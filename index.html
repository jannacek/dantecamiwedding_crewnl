Our base camp

What we're looking for

Chacras de Coria and its surroundings are full of quintas — big houses with gardens and pools. Shared between all of us, a great house costs less than mediocre hotel rooms. Booked Tue 15 → Mon 21 Dec, six nights.

Location

10–15 min by Uber

A short ride from Villa Elina — Ubers in Mendoza are cheap and plentiful, and we'll sort shared rides on the big nights so nobody's doing logistics at 4 a.m.

Essentials

Pool & parrilla

December in Mendoza is high-desert summer: ~30 °C and relentlessly sunny. A pool is non-negotiable. So is a proper grill.

Cost

Roughly €35–60 a night
